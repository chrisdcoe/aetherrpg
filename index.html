Welcome to the world of Aether

The World of Aether is a campaign setting in progress created for use in Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition. It is a very high-magic, high-fantasy sort of setting with an emphasis on exploration, heroism, and larger-than-life adventure!

The setting utilizes several customized races: some are familiar, others may be a little new. All of the standard classes are available for play in this world.

A World of Mysteries

Imagine a world of little islands in the sky hovering over an endless sea of clouds. Picture yourself standing on the deck of an airship, sailing through the sky toward your next adventure. You could be anyone, go anywhere, discover anything. Delve into ancient ruins from the world that came before. Battle against ferocious monsters and wicked men alike. The world is your oyster.

Join a Faction

Explore the world of Aether alongside one of many factions, each with their own motivations and goals. Will you be a member of the Explorers' Guild, seeking out new lands and relics of the past? Or perhaps you're more at home with the Mousaeum, a scholarly group studying the history of the world.
