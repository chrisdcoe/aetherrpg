Factions of Aether

There are many different groups in the Core Lands of Aether with different agendas. For some, the unexplored frontier holds promises of archaeological wonders and new civilizations to befriend. Others, seeking glory and fame, may be interested in artifacts and treasure.

But not everyone is so benign. Within the shadows lurk those who seek great power and will stop at nothing to achieve their ends.
